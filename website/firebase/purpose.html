Our Purpose


At its inception, DoveVest was created in order to bridge the gap between novice investors and the stock market. Today, it’s a world wide platform with the ability to assist new investors with the decisions necessary to have a positive return on their investment. Users can quickly type in their preferred company name and figure out whether it is a good buy based off of how our system returns the results. We use some of today’s fastest technologies in order to quickly scan and identify important data, actions, and interactions between users online.


Get text updates

We used Twilio to connect our data to our users. Once you subscribe to our text messages, you will receive text updates for the latest best stocks.

How it works

We are parsing through finance articles to analyze the public sentiment surrounding popular stocks in the stock market, and using the Google Natural Language API to process the data. Our website is constantly updated to show the most relevant and recent information, and we connect this to our text subscription system as well.
